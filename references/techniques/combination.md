# Conceptual Combination

## When to Use
Product innovation, feature ideation, business model design. When you need something genuinely new, not incremental.

## The Method
1. List 2-3 unrelated concepts, products, or domains.
2. Force them together: "What is the child of X and Y?"
3. Don't reject weird combinations immediately. Sit with them.
4. Extract the essence of each combination that works.
5. Refine into a concrete proposal.

## Example
Combination: "Task manager + RPG game"
Essence: Tasks have XP. Completing tasks levels you up. Boss battles = big deadlines. Streaks = combos. Party system = team projects.
Outcome: Gamified productivity app — not just badges, but actual game mechanics mapped to work.

## Why It Works
Innovation is rarely pure invention — it's recombination. Forcing two concepts together mimics how breakthrough products actually emerge (phone + computer = smartphone).
